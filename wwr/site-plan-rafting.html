Overview

Purpose

Our purpose sits deep in our hearts to show the excitement and thrills in taking an outdoor adventure into the wilderness. We want everyone to have a chance to experience this at an affordable price at least once in a lifetime.

Audience

We work with individuals at any age but have found that most of our clients are between 25 and 40 years. Our customers want an adventure. They want a change. They are still curious about life. Most of our customer love the outdoors and camping.
